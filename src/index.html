Behind the Scenes

Meet Nikolina

Nikolina Vujinović is an architect and interior designer based in Banja Luka. With a deep passion for spatial harmony, she specializes in creating modern, warm, and functional interiors. Her approach combines creativity with precision, using industry-standard tools like SketchUp, V-Ray, AutoCAD, and Enscape to bring ideas to life with clarity and realism.

From concept to detailed plans, Nikolina ensures that every space she designs reflects the personality and needs of the people living or working in it.

FAQs

Everything you need to know about our design services

Do you work online/remotely?

Yes, I work remotely and can assist you regardless of your location.

Can I request changes?

Absolutely. I welcome feedback and offer revisions to ensure you're fully satisfied.

What are your payment terms?

We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and offer flexible payment plans for larger projects.

How long does a project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity, but I’ll always provide a clear estimate up front.

Do you help with furniture sourcing?

Yes, I can assist with selecting and sourcing furniture that aligns with your design and budget.
